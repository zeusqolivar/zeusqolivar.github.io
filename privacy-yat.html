Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Thank you for using "You're Almost There" (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

The App requires access to your device's location services to function correctly. Specifically:

  • Location Data: We collect and process your location data (GPS) to determine your distance from your selected destination and trigger the alarm. This includes background location tracking when a trip is active.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide the core functionality of the App:

  • To track your progress toward your destination.
  • To alert you when you enter your specified proximity radius.

3. Data Storage and Third Parties

We use external services to help power the app:

  • Supabase: We may anonymously sync trip statistics to our backend (Supabase) to improve app performance. No personally identifiable information (PII) is attached to this data.
  • Google Maps & Places SDK: We use Google services to provide map views and location searches. By using the App, you are also bound by Googleâ€™s Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

Location data is processed in real-time. We do not maintain a permanent historical log of your movements linked to your identity.

5. Your Choices

You can revoke the App's access to your location at any time via your device's Settings. However, doing so will prevent the App from tracking your commute and triggering the alarm.
